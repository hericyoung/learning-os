# Learning OS Roadmap

This roadmap defines the long-term structure of Learning OS.

It is not a task list, but a structured map of how knowledge domains connect and evolve over time.

---

# Stage 1 — Foundations of Thinking

Focus:
Build fundamental models that appear across almost all disciplines.

Current Status: 🟢 Active

## Current Course

* Economics

## Core Models

* Scarcity
* Choice
* Opportunity Cost
* Incentives
* Rational Decision Making
* Behavioral Economics

Purpose:
Understand how decisions are made under constraints.

---

# Stage 2 — Human Behavior

Focus:
Understand how humans think, behave, and make decisions.

Status: ⚪ Planned

## Future Courses

* Psychology
* Cognitive Science

## Core Models

* Cognitive Biases
* Habit Formation
* Motivation Systems
* Feedback Loops
* Learning Mechanisms

Purpose:
Explain deviations from rational decision-making.

---

# Stage 3 — Systems & Complexity

Focus:
Understand how interactions between agents create emergent behavior.

Status: ⚪ Planned

## Future Courses

* Systems Thinking
* Complexity Theory
* Cybernetics

## Core Models

* Emergence
* Self-Organization
* Network Effects
* Stability & Fragility
* Adaptation

Purpose:
Understand how large systems behave beyond individual components.

---

# Stage 4 — Society & Strategy

Focus:
Understand collective behavior, institutions, and strategic interaction.

Status: ⚪ Planned

## Future Courses

* Sociology
* Political Science
* Institutional Economics

## Core Models

* Strategic Interaction
* Incentive Alignment

Understand how groups and societies coordinate or fail to coordinate.

# Stage 5 — Technology & Information

---

# Long-Term Integration Layer

All stages eventually converge into a unified model system:

* MODELS/

Where all reusable mental models are stored, refined, and connected across disciplines.

---

# Design Principle of the Roadmap

This roadmap is not linear in practice.

Learning is iterative:

* Concepts may appear across multiple stages
* Models are reused across disciplines
* Real-world observations may reshape the structure

The roadmap evolves with understanding.
